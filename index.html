Albert Park Circuit

4 — 9 March 2026 | Season Opening Race — Melbourne

Circuit Length 5.278 km
Race Distance 306.124 km
Corners 14 turns
Race Laps 58 laps
DRS Zones 4 zones
Attendance 465,000+ fans

Albert Park Circuit is a favourite on the F1 calendar and in 2026 it will again be the season opening race. Winding around a picturesque lake just three kilometres south of Melbourne's CBD, the circuit delivers faster lap times and significantly improved overtaking opportunities following its 2022 modifications.

Turn 1 — Jones

Heavy braking from 320 km/h. Prime overtaking zone into tight right-hander.

Turn 3 — Whiteford

Technical 90° corner requiring precise braking and engine control.

Turn 11 — Clark

Fastest corner at 225 km/h. Generates up to 3.5G lateral forces.

Turn 14 — Senna

Final corner. Exit speed is crucial for overtaking on pit straight.
